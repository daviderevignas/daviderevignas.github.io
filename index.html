About Me

I am a soft matter researcher working in the Biological, Soft-matter & Statistical Physics Group at the Department of Physics, University of Padua.

Previously, I worked with the Biological Physics Group at MPI PKS Dresden and the Soft Matter Theory Group at the Chemistry Department, University of Padua.

My research interests include soft matter, statistical physics, biological physics, and computational modelling of complex systems.
